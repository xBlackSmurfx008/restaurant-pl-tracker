## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do I need accounting knowledge to use this?**
> No! The Operations section (Dashboard, Sales, Recipes, Ingredients, Vendors) works without any accounting knowledge. The Accounting section is there for those who want it.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
> Yes, the app works on any device with a web browser. Tablet or desktop recommended for data entry.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
> Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. Only logged-in users can access your restaurant's information.

### Recipes & Costing

**Q: How do I handle recipes with sub-recipes?**
> Create the sub-recipe as its own menu item (you don't have to sell it), then add it as an ingredient to the main dish.

**Q: What's the Q-Factor?**
> A flat amount added to every plate for misc items you don't track individually (napkins, condiments, etc.). Usually $0.25-$1.00.

**Q: Why are my costs showing $0?**
> Check that your ingredients have prices entered and the unit conversion factor is set correctly.

### Sales & Reports

**Q: Can I edit past sales?**
> Yes, just select the date and update the quantities.

**Q: How far back does data go?**
> All your historical data is saved. You can run reports for any time period.

### Expenses

**Q: How do I categorize an expense that fits multiple categories?**
> Use the category that represents the majority of the expense, or split it into multiple expense entries.

**Q: Can I attach receipts?**
> Yes! Upload photos or PDFs of receipts to any expense.
